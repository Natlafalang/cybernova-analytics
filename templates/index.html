Ransomware

Financial Institution Recovery — Gaborone

A commercial bank suffered a ransomware attack affecting 40% of its internal systems. CyberNova's team contained the breach within 6 hours, recovered all encrypted data from clean backups, and implemented network segmentation to prevent recurrence.

A targeted spear-phishing campaign compromised credentials of 12 government officials. CyberNova performed a full credential audit, reset access controls, and delivered a department-wide phishing awareness programme.

An e-commerce SME was hit with a sustained DDoS attack causing 18 hours of downtime. CyberNova deployed traffic scrubbing, configured rate limiting, and integrated CDN-level DDoS protection, restoring service in under 2 hours.
